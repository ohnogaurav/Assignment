Assessment Framework

This tool is grounded in Attention Control Theory and the Executive Function framework, evaluating top-down regulation of cognitive resources.

Sustained Attention

Measures Tonic Alertness—the capacity to maintain voluntary focus on a single objective despite interest decay.

Distraction Resilience

Evaluates Inhibitory Control—the ability to suppress irrelevant environmental cues while processing info.

Task Switching Efficiency

Measures Cognitive Flexibility—the efficiency of shifting mental sets and the associated 'switch cost'.

Mental Endurance

Assesses Cognitive Stamina—the metabolic reservoir of mental energy available before ego depletion.

Psychometric Validity

Construct Validity: Each section isolated via distinct situational items.
Internal Consistency: Balanced reverse-coded items to mitigate acquiescence bias.
