Attention Span & Focus Test

Made by: Gaurav Kumar

Project Explanation

🎯 Aim

To assess attention span and focus in real-life situations using a structured psychological methodology.

🧠 Attributes Tested

  • Sustained Attention
  • Distraction Susceptibility
  • Task Switching
  • Mental Endurance

📋 Methodology

Self-report questionnaire utilizing a 5-point Likert scale (1 = Low, 5 = High).

📊 Standardization

Balanced coverage and equal distribution of questions across all behavioral categories for accurate interpretation.

Questionnaire

Please rate the following statements from 1 (Strongly Disagree / Low) to 5 (Strongly Agree / High).
